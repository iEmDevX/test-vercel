## Usage Guidelines

### Primary Actions
Use **web-green-500** (`#00A950`) for primary buttons and active states.

### Text Colors
- **Primary text**: `gray-900` for headings
- **Secondary text**: `gray-600` for body text
- **Muted text**: `gray-400` for captions and hints

### Background Colors
- **Page background**: `gray-50` for light backgrounds
- **Card background**: `white` for content containers
- **Hover states**: `gray-100` for subtle highlights

### Interactive States
- **Hover**: Darker shade of the base color
- **Focus**: Use `ring-2 ring-web-green-500` for focus indicators
- **Disabled**: `gray-400` with reduced opacity

## Accessibility

All color combinations meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards for color contrast:
- Text on white backgrounds has a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1
- Interactive elements have sufficient color contrast for visibility
- Color is not the only means of conveying information
